Methodology & scope

What's real vs. illustrative

The enforcement log is synthetic data generated to mirror the shape of a real T&S dataset (region × policy category × device × channel age × detection source, ~78K rows over 16 weeks). The anomaly is deliberately engineered so the RCA has a real, findable answer.

What this demonstrates
  • Designing a metrics framework with both volume and precision proxies
  • SQL-based anomaly detection with window functions
  • Multi-variable RCA to disentangle confounds
  • Executive-ready narrative, not just a chart dump
